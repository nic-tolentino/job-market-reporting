1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how TechMarket ("we", "our", or "the Project") collects, uses, and manages data. This is an open-source, non-profit personal project developed by [Your Name] to support the engineering communities in Australia and New Zealand. Our mission is to provide market transparency and reinvest any surplus revenue into community initiatives such as developer scholarships and GDG events.

2. Information We Collect
We handle two types of information:

A. Aggregated Job Data (Scraped)
We collect publicly available job listings from third-party platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, Seek). To protect individual privacy, our processing pipeline is designed to sanitize this data immediately.

What we keep: Job titles, company names, tech stacks, salary bands, seniority levels, and locations.

What we discard: We explicitly do not store personal identifiers found in listings, such as recruiter names, personal email addresses, or phone numbers.

B. User Data (Direct)
Currently, we collect minimal information from our visitors:

Log Data: Like most websites, we collect information that your browser sends (IP address, browser type, pages visited).

Direct Communication: If you contact us via email, we will hold your contact details solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

To provide a searchable directory of "The Now" in the tech job market.

To generate aggregated market insights (e.g., "Top 5 Technologies in Melbourne").

To maintain and improve the performance of the website.

Non-Commercial Intent: We do not sell personal data to third parties. Any revenue generated (e.g., through donations or boosted listings) is used to cover hosting costs or funded back into the community.

4. Legal Basis for Processing
Our processing of publicly available job data is based on Legitimate Interest. We believe that providing a centralized, transparent view of the job market provides significant value to the professional community that outweighs the minimal privacy impact of storing non-personal, factual job details.

5. Data Storage and Security
Hosting: Our services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

Sanitization: Personal identifiers are scrubbed before data is stored in our BigQuery database.

Security: We implement standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access or data loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights
Under the NZ Privacy Act 2020 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APP), you have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of any personal data we may hold about you.

Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate information.

Erasure: Request that we delete your data from our systems.

If you are a recruiter or job poster and wish for a specific listing to be removed or modified, please contact us at the email below.

7. Third-Party Links
Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., original LinkedIn job posts). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.

8. Cookies
We use minimal cookies for basic site functionality and analytics to understand how the community uses our tool. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.
